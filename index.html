Step 3
CapCut Assembly

Step-by-step guide to building your faceless video in CapCut.

Open CapCut
Free video editor · Mobile & Desktop
1
Import your B-roll clips
Tap New Project → select all your clips → arrange in order: hook B-roll first, then body, then CTA clip.
2
Trim to 45–60 seconds total
TikTok rewards completion rate. Shorter is better. Cut any clip that feels slow — if it bores you it bores them.
3
Add text overlays
Tap Text → paste your hook. Set font to Montserrat Bold, size 48–56pt, white colour, centre position. Add body points one at a time timed to each clip.
4
Record or add voiceover
Tap Audio → Voiceover to record your own. Or use Text-to-Speech for AI voice. Read the script slowly and with authority — don't rush.
5
Add background music
Tap Audio → Sounds. Search "luxury ambient" or "cinematic dark". Set volume to 8–15% — music should sit under the voiceover, not compete with it.
6
Enable auto-captions
Tap Text → Auto Captions. 85% of TikTok is watched without sound. Captions are not optional — they are essential.
7
Export settings
Tap Export → Resolution: 1080p · Frame rate: 60fps · Format: MP4. Never export at lower quality — TikTok compresses on upload anyway.
8
Save to camera roll
Always save locally before posting. If TikTok has an issue on upload you still have your master file.

Voiceover Options

Three paths to audio. Pick what fits your comfort level.

Your Own Voice
RECOMMENDED

Most authentic. Builds personal brand even faceless. Your Ritz London authority comes through in your tone.

Tips
· Record in a quiet room — bathroom tiles or closet work well for acoustics
· Speak slowly and with pauses — luxury content is never rushed
· Hold phone 20–30cm from mouth
· Record in CapCut using the Voiceover function directly
CapCut AI Voice

Built into CapCut. Free. Good for getting started without recording yourself.
